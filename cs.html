Cyber Security Center

To defend against advanced cyber security attacks, an advanced cyber security intelligent system is needed. The Center of Excellence in Cyber Security is building an integrated cyber security intelligent infrastructure for mobile security, web security, application security, network security, system security, and email security. Furthermore, the cyber security Center of Excellence (CoE) is used to help students develop skills while also encouraging academic research and ideas for the best cyber security initiatives. The goal of the Cyber Security Center of Excellence is to promote a cyber-safe and conducive environment for industry collaboration, close ability gaps, raise awareness, and foster innovation in this emerging technology sector. The Center of Excellence will also facilitate standardization and best practices for information security across industry domains, foster innovation, research, and development, and run some of the most advanced in-house training programs in Cyber Security Technologies.

Projects

Secure Supply Chain to provide integrity

VISU(VIT-AP Intelligent super Utility) is a 3D printed robot built from scratch using minimal design and simple yet sophisticated electronics. Backed by Arduino and Raspberry pi and customized so as to make it modular in terms of design and technology that expands its range of abilities. The robot comes equipped with Voice and Face recognition tech. The arms and torso are powered by high torque Servo motors that provide enough power to lift a baby. Powered by over 25 motors, the robot can effortlessly mimic human movement. Every piece of tech involved has been hacked, modified and customized to satisfy constraints that were once a limitation.
